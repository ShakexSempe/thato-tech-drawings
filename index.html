Curriculum

Sketching

Freehand drawing that is usually not intended as a finished work. In general, sketching is a quick way to record an idea for later use.

Instrument Drafting

All cases demand precision, accuracy, and attention to detail of high order.

Computer aided design

Drafting tasks automated and accelerated through the use of computer-aided design systems (CAD).

Engineering

Engineering drawings generally deal with mechanical engineered items, such as manufactured parts and equipment.

Manual drafting

Detailing of physical objects through use of a thorough understanding of geometry, trigonometry and spatial comprehension.

Assembly drawings

Illustrate how different parts go together, identify those parts by number, and have a parts list, often referred to as a bill of materials.

3D projection

Design technique used to display a 3D object on a 2D surface. Project a complex objects for viewing capability on a simpler plane.

Standards & conventions

Basic drafting paper: International standard (A4 and its siblings)
